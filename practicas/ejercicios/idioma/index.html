Telefónica Empresas

Telefónica Empresas provides communications services to the corporate sector (large companies and public administrations).

Telefónica Latinoamérica

All Telefónica's investments in the fixed telephone sector in Latin America are managed through Telefónica Latinoamérica, with its main activities in Argentina (TASA), Brazil (Telesp), Chile (CTC), Peru and Telefónica Larga Distancia Puerto Rico. The company also manages Advance (ISP in Argentina), Cable Mágico (cable TV in Peru) and CTI (carrier acquired in 2000 to handle integral management of international traffic between Telefónica's Latin American operators and the United States).

Telefónica Soluciones and Telefónica Internacional Wholesale Services (which offers international capacity services to other Operators) also belong to this business unit.
